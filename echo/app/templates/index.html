Dark echo-location maze game

Find the exit by sound, not sight.

Echo Escape is a web-based puzzle game where the player explores a hidden maze. Sound pulses briefly reveal walls, traps and the exit. The web app saves every result and ranks players by score, time and echo efficiency.
